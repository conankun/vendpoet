Poetry Vending Machine Instructions

Introduction

This manual tells you how to operate the Poetry Vending Machine. The Poetry Vending Machine will allow you to dispense a random poem at your will.

List of Materials

1. Internet compatible device
2. Internet connection

Directions

Step 1. Open a web browser and visit this link: http://conankun.us/vendpoet/
Step 2. Click the button “GET POEM” on the home page
Step 3. A random poem will be displayed for your enjoyment
Step 4. Enjoy!
Step 5. If you would like to read another poem, navigate back to the home page and repeat the steps.

Transportation

The link provided will work on all devices that have a sustainable internet connection.
